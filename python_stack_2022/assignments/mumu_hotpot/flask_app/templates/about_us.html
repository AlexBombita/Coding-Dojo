ABOUT US

The History of Mumu Hotpot began in August 2004, when Eiichi Mochizuki and Koji Kikura opened their first Hot Pot restaurant in San Mateo. The name, "Mumu Hotpot".It was named to offer customers the best Japanese style hot pot in the US. At Mumu Hotpot, we gained immense popularity for our mouthwatering thinly-sliced Wagyu style beef and our all natural homemade ponzu and sesame dipping sauces. Our customers also enjoy counter-style Hotpot, allowing them to have their own pots to cook at their own pace. This style was imitated by many other stores throughout the Bay Area, and is now common in this industry.
